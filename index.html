Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  2. 3.5 tsp of baking powder
  3. 1 tsp of salt
  4. 1 tbsp white sugar
  5. 1.25 cups of milk
  6. 1 egg

Steps

Step 1

In a large bowl, sift together the baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth

Step 2

Use a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour out or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
